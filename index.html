BECCA

BASE Evaluation Criteria for Climate Adaptation

Welcome to BECCA!

BECCA is a careful selection of criteria to evaluate climate adaptation policies and measures. It is meant to be used as a check list of topics and issues to be considered in evaluations of concrete adaptation interventions.

We do not suggest applying all available criteria but only a reasonable selection of them. Climate adaptation is highly context-specific and, therefore, there is no "one-size-fits-all" criteria set for the evaluation of climate actions.

We provide guidance on which criteria to use in which adaptation situations. On this basis, the user is free to tailor their own set of evaluation criteria with specific weights of certain dimensions depending on the context.

BECCA is a product of Task 2.3 of the BASE project. For more details please consult BASE report 2.3, available in the following page: http://base-adaptation.eu/publications.

Usage instructions:

  1. Classify your adaption case in terms of the 7 structural properties available below. Click the icon to find more information about the meaning of the property. If some of them doesn't apply to your adaption case, leave it blank.
  2. Click the "Show criteria" button at the bottom. This tool will find the most relevant criteria for your case

1) Sector (choose one or more)
2) Impact (choose one or more)
3) Perspective
4) Integration
5) Approach
6) Orientation
7) Setting
